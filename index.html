General Membership

  • Accesss to all of our classes
  • Beauty products available in the locker rooms (shampoo, conditioner, shower gel, hair spray, body lotion)
  • Towel service
  • 15 guest passes included
  • Pay month-to-month or upfront for a year to save money

Student Membership

  • Same ammenities provided as with general membership
  • Student ID is required to get approval for student discount
  • Valid only for the duration of educational programm
  • Membership can be put on hold for no extra fees during the school breaks

Family Membership

  • Save more by signing up as a family!
  • Day Care is included in the price
  • Kids get 50% off the general membership and always have an access to the day care room
  • Special lockers are available for young mothers

Towel Service

Towels of various sizes are available throughout the gym at no extra cost. Bath size towels are placed in the locker room and sports size towels are available throught the fitness floor.

Spa & Massage

Spa, massage, facials - we offer a wide assortment of services in our on-site spa salon to help you relax, improve your health and flexibility, and recover from injuries. Inquire about our special pricing for new members today!

Day Care

Various kids programs are available, from Kids' Zumba to Boot Camp. Day care services are available all the time during the operational hours at the gym to be convenient for any schedule.
